Pastoral Welcome

Welcome to Our Church Family

Welcome to Jesus True Will Ministry. We are a Christ-centered community rooted in the Word of God, committed to transforming lives through the Gospel. Whether you are near or far, our doors and hearts are open to you. Come as you are and experience the unfailing love of Jesus Christ.

✨ "The Lord has done great things for us, and we are filled with joy." — Psalm 126:3

Contact Pastor

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in faith, and serve the community with the love and compassion of our Lord and Savior.

Our Vision

A community transformed by the love of God, walking in truth, and shining as a beacon of hope to Kenya and the nations beyond.

Our Values

Faith in God's Word, Love for all people, Prayer as our foundation, Community in Christ, and Service to those in need.

Verse of the Day
📖
"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways acknowledge Him, and He shall direct your paths."

— Proverbs 3:5-6 (NKJV)

Trusting God's Direction

In our daily lives, we often face crossroads where human wisdom falls short. God's Word reminds us that when we surrender our understanding and acknowledge Him in every decision, He faithfully guides our steps. Today, take a moment to release your worries and trust that the Lord is working all things together for your good.

Let this be your prayer: "Lord, I trust You with my whole heart. Lead me in Your truth and teach me, for You are the God of my salvation. Give me wisdom to follow Your path, not my own."
